Key Issues

Range

  • Purchasing Low Frequency (LF) Reader with Passive Tags
  • LF has cheapest readers.
  • Approximate range is ~20cm

  • Investigating switch to active / semi-active tags
  • Extend Range up to 100 times
  • Difficult to find, few low volume suppliers

Real Time Communications

  • Three Communication layers
  • State depends on many endpoints
  • Aiming for less than 4 second end to end response time
